K-Scheme vs I-Scheme

Understand the key differences between K-Scheme and I-Scheme curricula to make informed decisions about your polytechnic education path.

MSBTE Curriculum Schemes

K-Scheme

K-Scheme is designed by MSBTE in line with NEP 2020, offering flexibility and skill enhancement focused on the latest industry requirements.

  • Aligned with National Education Policy 2020
  • Credit-based assessment system
  • Flexible curriculum structure
  • Focus on skill development
  • Industry-relevant project work
Explore K-Scheme

I-Scheme

I-Scheme curriculum integrates practical skills, project-based learning, and industry collaboration for career readiness in the digital age.

  • Industry collaboration in curriculum design
  • Emphasis on practical applications
  • Project-based assessments
  • Digital skills integration
  • Entrepreneurship development modules
Explore I-Scheme
